About Me

I have a habit of "wandering" so my manager found a way to make sure I stayed where I was supposed to.

I have been working at Rockaway Lanes since I was 17 years old, I am 25 now. I just needed a part-time job while in school and this fit with my schedule. Little did I know that this many years later I would still be working there. Naturally working there I have become a pretty good bowler. After bowling consistently for 2 years I finally shot a 300, a perfect game of all strikes. For that effort it received a 300 ring which is given by the USBC for your first achievement. I have met some good friends along the way while working at Rockaway Lanes and even though I do like it there, I am looking forward to graduating and getting a job in my field.
