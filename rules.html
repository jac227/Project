Rules of Bowling

These are the rules of bowling...okay not exactly the rules of the game, more of just the rules your local center will have.

On the surface bowling seems like a simple sport and in a way it is. The main rule most people already know is "do not cross the line".
That line is the foul line, and it is placed at the start of the lane. If any part of your body crosses that foul line you will hear a buzzing sound and receive a score of F meaning foul and is counts the same as a 0. A strike(X) in bowling is worth 10 pins plus your next 2 balls. So for example, if you strike on your first frame then in your second frame you get a 5 and a 3 that would mean for your first frames score you would get an 18. Now a spare(/) is worth 10 pins plus just the next ball you throw. So in our previous example if you got a spare instead of a strike your score would be 15 for the first frame as it is 10 + 5 = 15.
